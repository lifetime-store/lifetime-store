About Lifetime

Quiet premium essentials with lasting value.

Lifetime is a premium apparel and identity brand operated by FVA70K SERVICES. We create refined everyday essentials — tees, polos, hoodies, and curated daily wear — built around clean structure, durable fabric, and verifiable authenticity.

This website and store are operated by FVA70K SERVICES under the Lifetime brand.

What matters most
Verified originals

Every eligible piece can be checked through our verification flow using code or supported scan methods.

Controlled batches

Products are managed through structured batch and label-first workflows that support traceability after production and after sale.

Direct-to-customer

Ordering, tracking, support, and delivery updates stay inside a cleaner workflow designed for confidence instead of noise.

Built for repeat wear

We believe premium clothing should feel complete in the hand, on the body, and over time. No noise. No excess. Just confidence you can wear daily.

Lifetime focuses on cleaner proportions, dependable finishing, and a restrained visual identity. The standard is simple: if the product does not feel premium before the logo is noticed, it is not ready.

Our operating promise

  • Verified originals supported by the Lifetime verification tools
  • Direct ordering and tracked fulfillment
  • Structured support, delivery, and authenticity workflows
  • Product discipline designed for long-term customer trust
