Community and review guidelines

1. What is welcome

Helpful product reviews, fit notes, delivery experiences, authenticity comments, and respectful customer questions are welcome.

2. What is not allowed

  • Spam or repeated promotional messages
  • Harassment or hateful content
  • False authenticity claims without explanation
  • Impersonation of staff, brand, or other customers
  • Posting private contact or payment information

3. Moderation

Lifetime may hide, remove, or restrict content that harms customers, misuses the platform, or damages a safe and fair shopping environment.
